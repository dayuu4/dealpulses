🎯 Our Mission

DealPulses exists because finding a genuinely great deal takes time — time most people don't have. Every day, retailers quietly drop prices on hundreds of products, but those deals can disappear within hours. Most people miss them entirely.

Our mission is simple: surface the best deals, fast, with enough context that you can make an informed decision in seconds. We combine automated price monitoring with human curation to ensure every deal featured on DealPulses is actually worth your attention.

We cover tech deals (laptops, monitors, audio, gaming, phones, smart home), bank and credit card bonuses, and select deals in other high-value categories. Our goal is to be the one tab you check before making any significant purchase.

⚙️ How DealPulses Works

Behind every deal card is our automated deal radar — a custom-built system that monitors hundreds of sources simultaneously and filters out the noise.

1

Continuous Monitoring

Our DealRadar engine scans 161 RSS feeds from 55+ retailers and deal aggregators around the clock, every few hours.

2

Price Intelligence

We track price history for every product we monitor, so we can tell you whether today's "sale" price is actually a deal or just retail with a red tag.

3

Quality Filtering

Not every discount makes it to the site. We apply minimum discount thresholds and check community deal scores before featuring a deal.

4

Expiry Detection

Our system automatically checks whether featured deals are still live. Expired or sold-out deals are marked clearly so you're never chasing a ghost.

5

Human Review

Automated systems catch volume; human eyes catch nuance. We review top deals manually before they're featured prominently on the homepage.

6

Instant Publishing

Verified deals are published immediately. Our average deal is live on DealPulses within minutes of going public at a retailer.
