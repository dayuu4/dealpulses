📋 1. Information We Collect

DealPulses is a deal aggregation website. We collect minimal information to operate the site and improve your experience.

Information collected automatically:

  • Log data: When you visit DealPulses, our hosting provider (Netlify) automatically logs your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring page, and pages visited. This information is used for security and performance monitoring only.
  • Cookies: We and our advertising partners use cookies and similar tracking technologies. See Section 2 for details.
  • Analytics: We may use aggregated analytics (such as page view counts) to understand which deals perform well. This data is not linked to any individual user.

Information you voluntarily provide:

  • Email address: If you sign up for deal alerts or our newsletter, we collect your email address. You may unsubscribe at any time.
  • Comments: If we enable a commenting system (such as Disqus), that third-party service governs the data collected when you post a comment.

We do not collect your name, physical address, payment information, or government ID numbers.

🍪 2. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. DealPulses uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., session state, security tokens). These cannot be disabled.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense places cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests. These are set by Google, not by DealPulses directly.
  • Preference cookies: We may store lightweight preferences (such as deal category filters) in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Managing cookies: You can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect advertising functionality. You can also opt out of personalized Google ads at adssettings.google.com or visit optout.aboutads.info for broader opt-out options.

📢 3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

DealPulses uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC.

As part of serving ads, Google may:

  • Use cookies and web beacons to collect data about your visits to DealPulses and other websites
  • Use this data to serve advertisements based on your interests (interest-based advertising)
  • Collect information such as your IP address, device identifiers, and browser information

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to DealPulses and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use partner sites or apps, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

DealPulses does not control the specific advertisements served by Google and is not responsible for their content. The ads shown on DealPulses are managed through Google's advertising platform.

We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. Amazon, the Amazon logo, and Amazon.com are trademarks of Amazon.com, Inc., or its affiliates.

We may also participate in affiliate programs offered by other retailers (such as Best Buy, Newegg, eBay, and others via affiliate networks including Impact.com and CJ Affiliate). Affiliate links are marked with rel="nofollow sponsored" in our code.

Our editorial independence: Affiliate commissions do not influence which deals we feature or how we rank them. We source deals based on value and discount size, not on commission rates. We aim to showcase the best deals available regardless of affiliate relationship.

⚖️ 7. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate personal data.

  • Opt-out of advertising: Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings or your browser's cookie controls.
  • Unsubscribe: Unsubscribe from email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

California residents (CCPA): California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. DealPulses does not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

European residents (GDPR): If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@dealpulses.com.
